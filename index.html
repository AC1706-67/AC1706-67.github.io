The system is failing.

Mission

The system wasn't built
for us.

Recovery technology has always been designed for clinicians, administrators, and grant officers. The people actually doing the work — the peer specialists, the outreach workers, the people in recovery themselves — were an afterthought.

Anonymous Haven is different. Every tool in this platform was designed by someone who has sat on both sides of the table. Who knows what a peer specialist needs in the middle of a session. Who knows what it feels like to need help and not know where to turn.

This is technology built from the ground up, for the ground level.

MANO Token · In Development

What if your recovery
couldn't be taken away?

Right now, when someone completes treatment, gets discharged from probation, or reaches 30 days clean — that proof lives in a file somewhere. A bureaucrat's drawer. A system that can lose it, deny it, or disappear.

MANO changes that. A soulbound credential that belongs to the person who earned it. Non-transferable. Tamper-proof. Verified on-chain. No identity exposed.

"What if the work you did in recovery could follow you — not as a file in a bureaucrat's drawer, but as something you own. Something nobody can take away."

A year and a half ago, Andrés Chávez was computer illiterate. He learned to use AI just to help him type. Today he's built four production-grade recovery technology tools — HIPAA-compliant, 42 CFR Part 2-compliant, with multi-organization architecture, clinical assessment tools, and a blockchain credentialing system in development.

"I used to be called a problem.
Now I build solutions."

He works as a Peer Lead at Recovery Alliance El Paso's opioid drop-in center during the day. He codes at night. He co-facilitates MAT and Celebrate Recovery meetings, hosts family support groups using a CRAFT-based curriculum, and shows up every weekend at a detox facility — because he sees himself in the people he serves.

Anonymous Haven isn't a startup built in a garage by someone who read about the opioid crisis. It's built from inside it. That's the difference.
